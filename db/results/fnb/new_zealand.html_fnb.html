Christchurch FNBs holds a Free Monthly Film Night/FNBS Dinner on the first Wednesday of each month at the Media Club,191 Armagh Street,Christchurch at 7.30pm.
Christchurch FNBS organises a Really Free Market, Bi-Monthly at Latimer Square, currently the second Saturday of every second month. We now have a three wheeler bike and large bike trailer that are to be purpose built to use as a mobile kitchen.
Christchurch FNBs also does meals at events like Hiroshima Day,J-Day {day of action against prohibition of Cannabis & all drugs},ANZ Bank out of Iraq actions at ANZ banks. We also are attempting to have regular meals on the streets in Cashel Mall at lunch times on Friday but this is dependant on volunteers. We fed the Anti- Capitalist Art Exhibition called "Upping the ANTI".This event is a Benefit for FNBs and will also be our first BREW NOT BOMBS. We are open to ideas for actions and are keen to cook whenever we can.
