Palm Springs
We gather on the 2nd Sunday of each month at Sunrise Park which is located on the corner of Sunrise Way and Ramon Rd. We encourage people to follow us Facebook atwww.facebook.com/ FoodNotBombsPalmSprings to stay up to date. We accept donations of all kinds including prepared potluck style vegan/vegetarian food, non-perishable boxed/canned food, and essentials such as personal hygiene products. Everybody is welcomed to come share a meal in a positive community environment with us. Bring yourself, your friends, family, pets, instruments, blankets, chairs, signs, ideas, information to share, flyers, and positivity! Come for the good vibes, find out more, and meet other wonderful people! Food Not Bombs has no leaders, we make decisions based on consensus, so come get involved and help shape where we take this! for more info email us fnbpalmsprings@activist.com or contact Krystle 760-702-8770
