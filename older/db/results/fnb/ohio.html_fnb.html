West Cleveland
We cook every saturday at noon at the St. Luke's Episcopal Church, 7708 Lake Avenue in Cleveland. Feel free to come by and help us cook and clean up after cooking. Then, join us at 5 pm in market square where we will be serving food. If you cant make it on Saturdays, you can still help us to glean resources, find donors, and organize benefits. We also serve at many activist and community events when we aren't at market square. Contact us or come to meetings for more information.
