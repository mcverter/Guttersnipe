SUNDAY DINNER
Every Sunday evening around 6pm, Food Not Bombs shares a free veggie meal open to everyone at Occidental Park (in Pioneer Square). (206) 949-0322, email seattle-fnb@riseup.net
SATURDAY FREE MARKET
Another local FNB project is the Saturday Free Market (a.k.a. "Produce to the People, Right On!"). Each week, FNB members and individuals from Yesler Terrace -- a government housing complex -- collect food donated from local co-ops and distribute it at the Yesler Terrace Community Center (835 Yesler Way) at noon. seattle-fnb@riseup.net or call (206) 729-0384.
CASCADE FREE MARKET
The Food Not Bombs Free Market distributes organic produce every Sunday at 1:30 pm, in the park next to the Cascade People's Center. We have produce, baked goods, packaged, and deli foods, many of which are organic, and occasional animal products. All are welcome. 309 Pontius Ave North, Seattle, WA 98109
